Commercial • Greeley

Industrial facility

Through-fastened metal panels over new insulation, with box gutters and downspouts sized for roof area.

The manufacturing plant’s original roof leaked at skylight clusters and along the north gutter where ice had bent hangers. Operations required a solution before Q4 production peaks, so we prioritized weekend panel removal and weekday interior protection only where absolutely necessary.

We specified a structural metal panel over new blanket and rigid insulation to meet updated energy code, replaced skylight curbs entirely, and upsized box gutters with internal bracing for snow load.

Safety & production constraints

  • Fall-arrest anchors labeled for in-house PM use post job
  • No hot work inside odor-sensitive production line days
  • Forklift escort for boom lift moves on active dock
  • Daily LOTO coordination with plant electrician

Major work packages

  1. Demo: Remove panels, wet blanket, rusted purlin clips.
  2. Structure: Sister select purlins; replace skylight bucks.
  3. Envelope: Insulation, vapor careful at freezer wall junction.
  4. Finish: Panels, gutters, downspouts, touch-up, warranty.
