Simple 4-Step Process

FROM INSPECTION TO WARRANTY

Clear scope from the first visit through warranty — no surprises.

1

Free inspection

We visit on your schedule, walk the roof safely, and use drone photos so you see issues without climbing. Typical visit 45–60 minutes; inspection is free and you keep photos for insurance.

2

Detailed quote

Materials, labor, timeline, and cleanup in a fixed-price proposal. Line items — not vague allowances. Financing options when you need them.

3

Expert install

Most homes finish in 1–2 days. We protect landscaping, move patio items carefully, and magnetic-sweep for nails. Built for Colorado wind and hail.

4

Final walkthrough

We review flashing, vents, and gutters with you, then hand over signed warranty paperwork and a photo report of the finished roof.
